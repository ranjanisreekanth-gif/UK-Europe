Bhavarth Abhang Kirtan (English, Tamil, Marati)

Dr. Shri Raghunathdas Maharaj

A renowned Hari Kirtan exponent, Dr. Shri Raghunathdas Maharaj is the son and disciple of Shri Tukaram Ganapathi Maharaj hailing from Kadayanallur, India. He is highly distinguished for being the first person to teach Dnyaneshwari in Tamil for devotees across the world. His dedication to the Varakari Sampradaya earned him the Youth Icon Award from Maharashtra. For his exceptional research, he was honoured with the Chancellor's Award and the Best Thesis Award for his PhD, "Dnyanottarabhakti in Girvana Dnyaneshwari". Maharaj has conducted over 5,000 kirtans and pravachans globally.

Yaatra Pandari – UK & Europe 2026

We are blessed to invite everyone for “Yaatra Pandari UK & Europe 2026”, a series of Hari Kirtans and Pravachans across the UK and Europe.

During Hari Kirtan, selected Abhangs are sung and their meanings are explained in English, Marathi, and Tamil for the benefit of devotees.
