Bhavarth Abhang Kirtan

Dr. Shri Raghunathdas Maharaj

Dr. Shri Raghunathdas Maharaj, son and disciple of Shri Tukaram Ganapathi Maharaj, is a renowned Hari Kirtan exponent. He was the first person to teach Dnyaneshwari in Tamil for devotees across the world.

He has received the Youth Icon Award from Maharashtra for his service towards the Varakari Sampradaya. He holds a PhD in "Dnyanottarabhakti in Girvana Dnyaneshwari" and has been honored with the Best Chancellor's Award and the Best Thesis Award for his exceptional research work.
